TITLE: What Does A Digital Marketing Agency Do? A Step By Step Guide
SOURCE_URL: https://www.qualityresourcellc.com/blog/what-does-a-digital-marketing-agency-do/

SUMMARY:
A digital marketing agency provides comprehensive online marketing services to businesses that lack in-house expertise, helping them increase their online presence, generate sales-ready leads, and ultimately boost product and service sales through various digital channels.

KEY_POINTS:
- Increases organic traffic to business websites
- Generates sales-ready leads through targeted strategies
- Expands brand reach across multiple online platforms
- Improves overall marketing effectiveness from top to bottom
- Provides expertise in SEO, PPC, social media, and content marketing
- Acts as an extension of internal marketing teams

DETAILS:
Digital marketing encompasses a wide range of online marketing functions including search marketing, pay-per-click advertising (PPC), social media marketing, content marketing, and more. A digital marketing agency serves as a one-stop solution offering these services under one umbrella. These agencies understand inbound marketing, customer journeys, and search engine algorithms. They help businesses deliver the right message through the right online medium at the right time to move leads through the sales funnel. Services typically include Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Social Media Marketing (SMM), PPC Advertising, Content Marketing, Web Development, and App Development.

GOALS:
- Increase online presence and visibility
- Generate high-quality organic traffic
- Create sales-ready leads
- Expand brand reach across digital platforms
- Improve product and service sales
- Strengthen online customer connections

WHY_IMPORTANT:
Businesses need digital marketing agencies because effective digital marketing is time-consuming, complex, and potentially expensive to manage internally. Most companies lack the specific expertise across all digital channels and don't have the time to dedicate to comprehensive marketing efforts. Agencies provide specialized knowledge in website design, SEO, advertising, email outreach, and social media promotion that internal teams may lack, ultimately improving ROI and helping businesses grow online quickly.

DIFFERENCE_FROM_IN_HOUSE_MARKETING:
While in-house marketing teams may have limited expertise and time constraints, a digital marketing agency provides comprehensive specialized knowledge across all digital channels, acts as a dedicated extension of the marketing team, and identifies weaknesses in current marketing activities that internal teams might miss.
