TITLE: What Is Performance Marketing and Why It's Important?
SOURCE_URL: https://www.qualityresourcellc.com/blog/what-is-performance-marketing-and-why-its-important/ 

SUMMARY:
Performance marketing is an online advertising strategy where businesses pay only when specific results are achieved, such as leads, sales, traffic, or downloads. It's a fast-growing approach used by major businesses to attract customers without upfront budget limitations.

KEY_POINTS:
- Payment happens only after measurable action is achieved
- No monthly or upfront payments required
- Lower financial risk than traditional advertising
- Easy to track and measure buyer journey
- Helps increase sales and brand reach
- Reduces wasted budget and increases ROI

DETAILS:
Performance marketing refers to online marketing programs where advertisers pay only when desired goals are reached. These goals include lead generation, product sales, website traffic, app downloads, and brand awareness. The strategy works through collaboration with paid advertising and brand ambassadors. Companies can track the entire click-to-consume path of each buyer and identify which platforms produce better results. Since payment is result-based, there's no budget loss or risk involved.

GOALS:
- Lead generation
- Increase product purchase and sales
- Application downloads
- Build brand awareness
- Attract new buyers
- Engage with customers

WHY_IMPORTANT:
Performance marketing allows businesses to increase sales, build brands, spread product awareness, and engage customers without budget limitations. It helps scale reach and conversion of new buyers at lower risk. Companies can reduce budget waste, increase brand revenue, and have clear assistance on where to invest more. The entire process is measurable and trackable from click to consumption.

DIFFERENCE_FROM_DIGITAL_MARKETING:
While both use online channels for promotion, digital marketing typically involves fixed monthly costs or upfront payments, whereas performance marketing only requires payment once the desired outcome or action has been achieved.

FAQ:
Q: What is performance marketing?
A: It is an online marketing strategy where advertisers pay only once a desired goal has been achieved, such as leads, sales, traffic, or downloads.

Q: Why do businesses prefer performance marketing?
A: Because it reduces financial risk, eliminates upfront costs, allows budget flexibility, and ensures payment only happens after results are delivered.

Q: How does performance marketing work?
A: It works through paid advertising and brand ambassadors, where businesses only pay when specific desired results are fulfilled rather than paying monthly or upfront.

Q: What are the main goals of performance marketing?
A: To generate leads, increase product purchases and sales, drive application downloads, build brand awareness, and attract new buyers.

Q: What makes performance marketing easy to track?
A: Companies can see the complete click-to-consume path of each buyer and identify which platforms are producing better results.
