Q: What is the difference between PPC and SEO?
A: PPC (Pay-Per-Click) delivers immediate visibility through paid advertisements at the top of search results, while SEO (Search Engine Optimization) builds organic rankings over time. PPC offers instant traffic and precise targeting, making it ideal for immediate results and specific promotional campaigns.
Q: How do you track the success of PPC campaigns?
A: We use continuous performance monitoring and data-driven insights to track key metrics including click-through rates, conversion rates, cost per acquisition, and return on ad spend (ROAS), providing transparent reporting on campaign performance and ROI.
Q: Do you handle both campaign setup and ongoing management?
A: Yes, our services include comprehensive campaign setup including keyword research, ad copy creation, audience targeting configuration, and landing page optimization, followed by continuous monitoring and refinement to ensure sustained performance.
Q: How quickly can I see results from PPC advertising?
A: Unlike SEO which takes months, PPC can deliver immediate visibility and traffic once campaigns are launched. However, we typically recommend a 2-4 week learning period to optimize targeting and bidding for maximum efficiency and ROI.
